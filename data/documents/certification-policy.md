# Cloud Security Certification Policy

This document contains fictional demo policy information for the
Enterprise Learning Copilot.

## Required Courses

Employees must complete all four required courses:

1. Cloud Security Fundamentals
2. Identity and Access Management
3. Secure Cloud Networking
4. Cloud Incident Response

## Assessment

The final assessment contains multiple-choice and scenario-based
questions.

The passing score is 80 percent.

Assessment scores should be calculated by deterministic application
logic rather than estimated by an AI model.

## Retake Policy

An employee who does not pass must wait at least 24 hours before another
attempt.

The employee should review the topics associated with incorrect answers
before retaking the assessment.

A maximum of three attempts is permitted within a 30-day period.

Additional attempts require manager approval.

## Certification Validity

The certification is valid for two years from the completion date.

Employees must complete recertification training before the
certification expires.

## Manager Reporting

Managers may view:

- Whether an employee has started the certification
- Completed required courses
- Assessment completion status
- Final pass or fail status
- Certification expiration date

Managers should not receive detailed private learning conversations.
